Welcome to Moto Madness!


At Moto Madness we are the only store specialising in reconditioning factory second motorcycles from the Big Four Japanese manufacturers. We turn what would be production waste into your dream machines at a heavy discount on average showroom prices.

All bikes are imported new directly from Japan with 0 mileage. Our team of expert mechanics and engineers carefully craft the machines into a state of perfect roadworthiness and also ensure that all bikes are compliant with EU safety standards and emissions regs.
